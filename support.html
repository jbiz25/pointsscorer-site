Points Scorer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Points Scorer does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal information with anyone.

All game data is stored locally on your device. When you explicitly choose to share a live scoreboard with other players, that game is synced via Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) only with the specific iCloud users you invite — the data never touches our servers. Nearby-device sync uses Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework and stays on your local network.

Points Scorer does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking.
